Bio

Since a young age I have been infatuated with technology. I see computers as revolutionary tools which have the power to transform all aspects of society. My goal is to master this tool and build things others find useful. My passion for technology has led me to study full stack web development at CNM Ingenuity's Deep Dive Coding Camp. Deep Dive is a full time (400 hours), remote and totally immersive fullstack development experience. I'm grateful to have the opportunity to learn from so many talented peers, instructors and alumni.

The following are a few of the technologies I have studied and used in my projects.

Front End Development : Javascript, React, Bootstrap, HTML5, CSS3
Back End Development : Node.js, MySQL, Express.js, Typescript, Docker
UI/UX Design : Figma, ERD Visualization, REST API Diagrams, Dieter Rams' principles of good design.
Project Management Tools : Git Version Control, GitHub, Agile Project Management

Personally, I have a wide range of interests including but not limited to new technologies, geopolitics, books, finance, music and the outdoors. I am the son of two immigrant parents and a proud Dreamer - born in Colima, MX and raised in New Mexico. This has contributed to my worldview significantly, I consider myself to be an open-minded, creative and passionate individual. I enjoy teaching myself new skills and sharing a warm cup of coffee with friends. Feel free to reach out if you have any questions or are just looking to say hi!
