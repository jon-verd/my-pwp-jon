Jonathan Verduzco's PWP

Purpose, Audience, Goal

Purpose:

This project is going to be a professional portfolio showcasing my website building ability.

Audience:

The target audience for my personal website will be professional peers as well as prospective employers and clients.

Goal:

My primary goal is to make a beautiful website that will showcase my skills and projects. This website will also be set up to showcase my work to potential employers, clients and my peers.

Persona

Name: Jay Bird

Age: 34

Profession:

Jay Bird is a Senior Developer at SuperEvilMegaCorp. His team is looking for a junior web developer to assist with their website building.

Technology:

Jay Bird uses linux for his operating system and Mozilla Firefox is his preferred browser. He also has a M1 Macbook that he takes everywhere. His mobile device of choice is an iPhone 11.

Attitudes and Behaviors:

Jay is patient and detail oriented. He likes to take the time to look through websites of potential candidates. Jay also likes to do extensive research on their candidates throughout the web.

Frustrations and Needs:

Jay wants a talented young programmer that can help do some of the more simple and repetitive tasks taking up his time. Jay wants to use the time this would give him to create more complex programs. Jay wants somebody who is a cultural fit for SuperEvilMegaCorp. Jay Bird is local to Albuquerque and is looking for somebody who lives in the area but is having difficulties finding junior developers.

Goals:

Jay wants to start creating his new program in April. His company's deadline for hiring the developer would be late March. Jay likes to meet potential candidates in person.

User Story:

Jay is looking for local talent that can help alleviate some of his teams 'heavy lifting'.
